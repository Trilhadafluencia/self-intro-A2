Self-Introduction: Garr Reynolds

Video Class Level A2

Shadowing Practice

Objectivo: Oiça e repita. Tente falar ao mesmo tempo que o Garr para melhorar o seu ritmo e entonação.

[Transcription]

"Hello everybody, my name is Garr Reynolds and I am a professor at Kansai Gaidai University. I also work as a business communications consultant and I travel all around the world and all around Japan giving presentations and helping companies such as Apple and Microsoft and Amazon and so on with their presentations."

"I've written five books about presentations, you can see some of them here. Before I joined Kansai Gaidai in 2003, I worked for Apple in Silicon Valley. Before that, I worked for Sumitomo Electric Industries in Osaka."

"Being a father of two children, one dog, and one cat, and two birds means I'm pretty busy most of the time. I love hiking and rugby and American football, and playing music. I'm a drummer, but I can also play piano and bass guitar a little bit. All right, so let's jump sometime."

Comprehension Quiz

Goal: Test your knowledge about Garr's facts.
1. Garr Reynolds is a professor at which university?
2. How many books did he write?
3. What is Garr's main instrument?
4. In which year did he join the university?
5. How many birds does he have?
